Rules

The Y Recharge Challenge is a game of chance where members of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities can complete pre-defined activities for chances to win a prize. The Y Recharge Challenge runs from midnight Central Standard Time on March 8, 2017 until 11:59pm Central Standard Time on March 21, 2017. After this time, no further entries to the promotion will be permitted.

No promotional fees or purchase required.

Member ID and an e-mail address must be entered and verified on the Y Challenge website when registering.

Participation is based on the member's ID number. Members that lose their card and are issued a new ID number will not be given credit for previous activity.

Visits completed or daily web tips collected outside of the timeframe for the Y Recharge Challenge do not earn chances to win prizes.

Who can participate

All active members of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities ages 10 and up are eligible to participate — including all membership types and all payment types.

If you are under 13 years old, we require your parent or guardian's YMCA email address so that we may notify them of your use of the Y Recharge Challenge in compliance with Childrenâ€™s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) laws.

Employees and family of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities are eligible to participate and can earn four (4) guest passes, but are not eligible to win Y Bucks prizes during the promotion.

How to participate

You will need your member ID to sign up to participate:

  • At www.ymcamn.org/challenge

  • In the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities mobile app

  • In a YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities branch location

The Y Recharge Challenge requires members of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities to meet a personalized visit goal, based on past behavior to earn four (4) guest passes.

Additionally, participants can collect one (1) bonus entry daily by viewing a website tip on the day it is posted. Each tip is attached to one (1) entry into the game of chance drawing for 24 hours. Although tips might be available to review on the site for more than 24 hours, an entry is only submitted if participants view the tip within the first 24 hours of it being posted online.

Tips and instructions appear on the Instant Win tab of the www.ymcamn.org/challenge site.

About the Prizes

Earned guest passes are automatically credited to member accounts on or before April 1, 2017. Guest passes must be used by December 31, 2017.

For each daily web tip collected, participants will receive one (1) entry into a random drawing at their local YMCA. Each YMCA location will have 34 total game of chance winners, including:

  • One (1) $100 Y Bucks certificate

  • Three (3) $50 Y Bucks certificates

  • Thirty (30) $25 Y Bucks certificates

General

No responsibility can be accepted for entries not received for whatever reason.

The YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities reserves the right to cancel or amend the promotion and these terms and conditions without notice.

No cash alternative to the prizes will be offered. Youth under the age of 18 can transfer their prize to a parent/guardian who is a member of the Y; all other prizes are not transferable.

Registration into the promotion will be deemed as acceptance of these terms and conditions.
