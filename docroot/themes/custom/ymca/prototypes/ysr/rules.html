    July 25 - August 16

    Y Games 2016

    Terms and Conditions

    Registration

    Promotion is open to all members who register on the Y Games 2016 microsite.

    No promotional fees or purchase required.

    Facility access ID and an e-mail address must be entered and verified on the Y Games microsite when registering.

    Participation is based on the member’s facility access ID number. Members that lose their card and are issued a new ID number will not be given credit for previous activity. Members will need to re-record their activity using their new ID number within the promotion period.

    Participation

    Component 1: Visit Goal Achievement

    Participants are eligible to receive two (2) guest passes for reaching a visit goal (visiting the Y a predetermined amount of times during the promotion period).

    Visit goals are based on an individual member’s visit history in the weeks leading up to the promotion period (May 30 – July 22) with a small stretch added. Participants without complete visit history (new members, those that did not visit more than once per week, etc.) will be assigned a goal based on average usage rates. Visit goals will be given at registration on the Y Games promotion microsite.

    Visits are automatically tracked by swiping the facility access ID at check in. Limit of one visit tracked per day per promotion period.

    Guest passes will be added to the participant’s account within fourteen (14) days of the promotion close.

    Component 2: Game of Chance

    Participants are also eligible for a random drawing prize. Entries are assigned based on four criteria.

    Visit Goal Achievement.

    A member earns one (1) entry (automatically) for achieving their visit goal. Visits are automatically tracked by swiping the facility access ID at check in. Limit one entry per promotion.

    Record Activities*.

    Visit the Y Games promotional microsite and record an activity within any or all of the three (3) categories:

    a. Swim
    b. Group Exercise
    c. Fitness Center

    *Members will earn one entry per category per promotion period.
    Recording multiple activities does not increase your chances of winning in the random drawing. Members can visit any branch during the promotion period to track their activity and visits. Members will be entered into the random drawing at their home branch only.

    Promotion runs Wednesday, August 3, 2016 through Tuesday, August 16, 2016. Closing date for entry will be the final day of the promotion at 11:59pm Central Time. After this time, no further entries to the promotion will be permitted.

    Employees and family members on an employee membership are not eligible for the game of chance portion of the promotion.

    Prizes

    Each branch will award (4) gold prize package winners, four (4) silver prize package winners, and four (4) bronze prize package winners per promotion.

    Gold Prize
    • One (1) gold medallion
    • One (1) free month of membership at the Y in September 2016
    • Three (3) months of towel service* at the Y in September, October, and November
    • One (1) Hydro Flask water bottle.

    Limit four (4) winners per branch. One winner for each track (swim, group x, and fitness) and one winner for achieving the visit goal. Membership award is based on the member’s current membership type.

    Silver Prize
    • One (1) silver medallion
    • Three (3) months of towel service* at the Y in September, October, and November
    • One (1) Hydro Flask water bottle.

    Limit four (4) winners per branch. One winner for each track (swim, group x, and fitness) and one winner for achieving the visit goal.

    Bronze Prize
    • One (1) bronze medallion
    • One (1) Hydro Flask water bottle

    Limit four (4) winners per branch. One winner for each track (swim, group x, and fitness) and one winner for achieving the visit goal.

    * Members with complimentary towel service (as part of their normal membership) will receive a Y-branded towel in place of the 3 months of towel service. This substitution does not apply to members who pay a monthly fee for towel service.

    Winner Selection & Notification

    Prize winners will be selected and notified within fourteen (14) days of the promotion close. Winners are limited to one game of chance prize package per member per promotion.

    Winners will be chosen from a random draw of entries received in accordance with these Terms and Conditions. The draw will be performed by a random computer process.

    Winner’s names will be posted within fourteen (14) days after the promotion has been completed on the Y Games promotion microsite.

    Winners will be notified via e-mail or phone within fourteen (14) days of the announcement. If the winner cannot be contacted or does not claim prize within fourteen (14) days of notification, we reserve the right to withdraw the prize from the winner and pick a replacement winner.

    The winner will be able to pick up any physical prize(s) in their recorded branch.

    General

    No responsibility can be accepted for entries not received for whatever reason.

    The YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities reserves the right to cancel or amend the promotion and these terms and conditions without notice.

    No cash alternative to the prizes will be offered. Youth under the age of 18 can transfer their prize to a parent/guardian who is a member of the Y; all other prizes are not transferable.

    This promotion is in no way sponsored, endorsed or administered by, or associated with the Olympics. You are providing your information to the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities and not to any other party.

    Registration into the promotion will be deemed as acceptance of these terms and conditions.
