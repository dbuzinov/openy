Y Spirit rules

The Y Spirit Challenge is a game of chance where members of the YMCA of the
Greater Twin Cities can complete pre-defined activities for a chance to win a prize.
The Y Spirit Challenge runs from midnight on December 1, 2016 until 11:59pm
on December 14, 2016.

Activities completed outside of the timeframe for the Y Spirit Challenge do not earn
chances to win prizes.

Who can participate

All active members of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities ages 10 and up are
eligible to participate—including all membership types and all payment types.

Staff members of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities are eligible to participate, but
are not eligible to win prizes during the promotion.

How to participate

You will need your facility access ID to sign up to participate:

  • At www.ymcamn.org/challenge
  • In the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities mobile app
  • In a YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities branch location

When you complete any of the following activities, you will receive a code for a
chance at an instant-win prize:

  • Register for the Y Spirit Challenge (earn one code when you sign up)
  • Check in at the YMCA (earn one code per day that you visit the Y)
  • Complete select activities and track them online (earn one code per day that
    you complete and report activities, regardless of the number of activities
    tracked)

Eligible activities include:

  • Using the lap pool
  • Using the warm-water leisure pool
  • Using the splash deck
  • Using the whirlpool
  • Using the water slide
  • Using the sauna
  • Using the steam room
  • Using the vortex pool
  • Participating in, or taking kids to Swim Lessons
  • Using a cardio machine
  • Using a strength machine
  • Using weights
  • Using the indoor track
  • Using the Youth/Family Fitness Cards
  • Using the climbing wall
  • Participating in Open Gym
  • Playing racquetball or handball
  • Participating in any Personal Training program, including: (one-on-one personal training, buddy training, small-group training, flex training, personal
    consultation, fitness assessment, metabolic assessment or equipment orientation)
  • Taking kids to Youth Sports
  • Participating in any Group Exercise, (water Exercise, Kids & Family or Active Older)
  • Adults class
  • Asking about volunteering at the Y
  • Asking about birthday parties at the Y
  • Attending a YMCA holiday party
  • Attending an Active Older Adult event
  • Dropping kids off at Kids Stuff
  • Registering for Parents Night Out
  • Visiting a Y Senior Center
  • Visiting a Y Youth & Teen Center

Instant-win codes and instructions appear on the Instant Win tab of the
www.ymcamn.org/challenge site.

About the Prizes

Winning codes are delivered randomly—and are not impacted by the number or type
of activities completed during the Y Spirit Challenge.

Prizes include digital gift cards valued from $5 to $100 each. Gift cards are
delivered through Tango Cards, where winners can redeem at retailers including:

  • Amazon
  • AMC
  • Barnes & Noble
  • Best Buy
  • CVS Pharmacy
  • Dick’s Sporting Goods
  • Google Play
  • iTunes
  • Target
  • Whole Foods
