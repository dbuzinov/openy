Y Spirit rules

The Y Spirit Challenge is a game of chance where members of the YMCA of the
Greater Twin Cities can complete pre-defined activities for a chance to win a prize.
The Y Spirit Challenge runs from midnight on December 1, 2016 until 11:59pm
on December 14, 2016.After this time, no further entries to the promotion will be permitted.

No promotional fees or purchase required.

Member ID and an e-mail address must be entered and verified on the Y Spirit Challenge
website when registering.

Participation is based on the member’s ID number. Members that lose their
card and are issued a new ID number will not be given credit for previous activity.
Members will need to re-record their activity using their new ID number within
the promotion period

Activities completed outside of the timeframe for the Y Spirit Challenge do not earn
chances to win prizes.

Who can participate

All active members of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities ages 10 and up are
eligible to participate—including all membership types and all payment types.

Staff members of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities are eligible to participate, but
are not eligible to win prizes during the promotion.

How to participate

You will need your member ID to sign up to participate:

  • At www.ymcamn.org/challenge
  • In the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities mobile app
  • In a YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities branch location

When you complete any of the following activities, you will receive a chance for a
chance at an instant-win prize:

  • Register for the Y Spirit Challenge (earn one chance when you sign up)
  • Check in at the YMCA (earn one chance per day that you visit the Y)
  • Complete select activities and track them online (earn one chance per day that
    you complete and report activities, regardless of the number of activities
    tracked)

Eligible activities include:

  • Using the lap pool
  • Using the warm-water leisure pool
  • Using the splash deck
  • Using the whirlpool
  • Using the water slide
  • Using the sauna
  • Using the steam room
  • Using the vortex pool
  • Participating in, or taking kids to Swim Lessons
  • Using a cardio machine
  • Using a strength machine
  • Using weights
  • Using the indoor track
  • Using the Youth/Family Fitness Cards
  • Using the climbing wall
  • Participating in Open Gym
  • Playing racquetball or handball
  • Participating in any Personal Training program, including: (one-on-one personal training, buddy training, small-group training, flex training, personal
    consultation, fitness assessment, metabolic assessment or equipment orientation)
  • Taking kids to Youth Sports
  • Participating in any Group Exercise, (water Exercise, Kids & Family or Active Older)
  • Volunteered
  • Worked out outside
  • Walked to the bus
  • Biked to work
  • Took stairs instead of elevator
  • Did sit ups during TV commercial
  • Did household chores
  • Family park day
  • Family bike ride
  • Coached a sports team

Instant-win chances and instructions appear on the Instant Win tab of the
www.ymcamn.org/challenge site.

About the Prizes

Winning chances are delivered randomly—and are not impacted by the number or type
of activities completed during the Y Spirit Challenge.

Prizes include digital gift cards valued from $5 to $100 each. Gift cards are
delivered through Tango Cards, where winners can redeem at retailers including:

  • Amazon
  • AMC
  • Barnes & Noble
  • Best Buy
  • CVS Pharmacy
  • Dick’s Sporting Goods
  • Google Play
  • iTunes
  • Target
  • Whole Foods

General

No responsibility can be accepted for entries not received for whatever reason.

The YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities reserves the right to cancel or amend
the promotion and these terms and conditions without notice.

No cash alternative to the prizes will be offered. Youth under the age of 18 can transfer
their prize to a parent/guardian who is a member of the Y; all other prizes
are not transferable.

This promotion is in no way sponsored, endorsed or administered by, or associated
with the Olympics. You are providing your information to the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities
and not to any other party

Registration into the promotion will be deemed as acceptance of these terms and conditions.
