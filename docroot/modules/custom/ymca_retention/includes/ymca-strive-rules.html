Rules

The Y Strive Challenge is a game of chance where members of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities can complete pre-defined activities for a chance to win a prize. The Y Strive Challenge runs from midnight on August 1, 2017 until 11:59pm on August 14, 2017. After this time, no further entries to the promotion will be permitted.

No promotional fees or purchase required.

Member ID and an email address must be entered and verified on the Y Strive Challenge website when registering.

Participation is based on the member’s ID number. Members that lose their card and are issued a new ID number will not be given credit for previous activity.

Visits completed or activities tracked outside of the timeframe for the Y Strive Challenge do not earn chances to win prizes.

Who can participate

All active members of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities ages 10 and up are eligible to participate — except for those with summer and Get Summer (seasonal teen) memberships.

If participants are under 13 years old, we require an email address for the participant’s parent or guardian so that we may notify them of the participant’s use of the Y Strive Challenge in compliance with Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) laws.

Employees and family of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities are eligible to participate and can earn four (4) guest passes, but are not eligible to win prizes during the promotion.

How to participate

Participants will need your member ID to sign up:

  • At www.ymcamn.org/challenge

  • In the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities mobile app

  • In a YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities branch location

The Y Strive Challenge requires members of the YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities to meet a personalized visit goal, based on past behavior to earn four (4) guest passes.

Additionally, participants can collect up to four (4) entries daily during the promotion for a random prize drawing. To receive entries for the prize drawing, participants complete pre-defined activities (list below) in the categories of Swimming, Group Exercise, Fitness and Community, and then report these activities on the www.ymcamn.org/challenge site.

Eligible activities include:

  • Using the lap pool

  • Using the leisure pool

  • Using the splash deck

  • Using the whirlpool

  • Using the water slide

  • Using the sauna

  • Using the steam room

  • Using the vortex pool

  • Participating in, or taking kids to Swim Lessons

  • Using a cardio machine

  • Using a strength machine

  • Using weights

  • Using the indoor track

  • Using the Youth/Family Fitness Cards

  • Using the climbing wall

  • Participating in Open Gym

  • Playing racquetball or handball

  • Participating in any Personal Training program, including: one-on-one personal training, buddy training, small-group training, flex training, personal consultation, fitness assessment, metabolic assessment or equipment orientation

  • Taking kids to Youth Sports

  • Participating in any ForeverWell, Group Exercise, Water Exercise, or Kids & Family class

  • Biking for transportation (e.g., to work, to the bus stop, to run errands)

  • Coaching a team

  • Exercising during TV commercial

  • Taking a family bike ride

  • Having a family park day

  • Doing household chores

  • Doing an outdoor workout

  • Taking the stairs instead of an elevator

  • Volunteering

  • Walking for transportation (e.g., to work, to the bus stop, to run errands)

About the Prizes

Earned guest passes are automatically credited to member accounts on or before August 31, 2017. Guest passes must be used by December 31, 2017.

Participants can collect up to four (4) entries daily during the promotion for a random prize drawing at their local YMCA location on August 31, 2017. Each YMCA location will have 12 total game of chance winners, including a first-, second- and third-prize winner for each of the following categories:

  • Aquatics

  • Group Exercise

  • Fitness

  • Community

First prizes include:

  • One (1) free month of YMCA membership*

  • Three (3) free months of towel service**

  • One (1) Y water bottle

Second prizes include:

  • Three (3) free months of towel service**

  • One (1) Y water bottle

Third prizes include:

  • One (1) Y water bottle

*Contract members (including participants in the Silver&Fit, SilverSneakers and MHP programs) who do not pay membership fees will receive $68 in Y Bucks (equal to one month of adult membership fees) in place of the free month of membership. This substitution does not apply to members who pay for membership.

**Members with complimentary towel service (including those at the Hudson YMCA and Corporate Wellness memberships) will receive a YMCA beach towel in place of the free months of towel service. This substitution does not apply to members who pay a monthly fee for towel service.

General

No responsibility can be accepted for entries not received for whatever reason.

The YMCA of the Greater Twin Cities reserves the right to cancel or amend the promotion and these terms and conditions without notice.

No cash alternative to the prizes will be offered. Youth under the age of 18 can transfer their prize to a parent/guardian who is a member of the Y; all other prizes are not transferable.

Registration into the promotion will be deemed as acceptance of these terms and conditions.
